Introduction

In May 2025, Blatten witnessed an unprecedented glacier collapse. Our platform tracked early warning signals using SAR data.

Early Warning Signals

Satellite imagery showed accelerated glacier velocity exceeding 2.5 m/day, a red flag for instability.

The Collapse

On May 30th, the glacier collapsed, impacting nearby villages. Rescue operations were launched immediately.

Community Response

Local authorities evacuated zones, set up shelters, and coordinated with international disaster teams.

Future Resilience

With real-time SAR monitoring, communities can prepare earlier for similar events.
